About Us

When do courses start?

New classes start every Autumn, Spring, and Summer term. Terms are subdivided into mini-courses which focus on a single topic and last three to five weeks each. A schedule of upcoming mini-courses and their responsible teachers can be found here.

How do I register?

Currently, we are running our classes either online or as in-school programs. If you would like us to come to your school, let us know!

What is your child safeguarding policy?

We take responsibility for the safety and well-being of all children and young people attending the London Maths Outreach activities. We follow the University College London guidelines for Working with Vulnerable Groups.

If any incident causes concern in respect of the welfare of a child or young person, it must be reported. It can be reported to our designated child safeguarding officer Dr Luciano Rila.

What is your data protection policy?

We take the collection and handling of any data you trust with us very seriously, and do not hold on to it any longer than we need to. More specific information can be found here.
