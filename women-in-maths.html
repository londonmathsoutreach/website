Women in Mathematics

London Maths Outreach wants to foster a fair, diverse, supportive, and fun environment for students in London to learn and enjoy mathematics. We aim to counteract the underrepresentation of women in mathematics and the mathematical sciences by especially encouraging young women to register for our courses and participate in our program, and by specifically targeting girlsâ€™ schools in London. We are delighted to say that we have received a large number of registrations from female students in recent times, and we hope this can be an encouraging fact for prospective students. We promise to put in the work to keep this trend going, and look forward to welcoming a diverse cohort every term.
